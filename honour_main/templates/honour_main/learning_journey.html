Honour Learning Journey

Harnessing the power of film in values education

Highly rated by students as an experiential, inspiring and ‘never boring’ programme, the Honour Learning Journey showcases the most moving Honour films to inspire students toward honour, respect, integrity, compassion and more.

More than 300 students have been inspired to take positive action in their family, school and community with guidelines from the HGToolkit(Honour Ghana Toolkit). Contact us to arrange a Learning Journey for your school, and harness the power of film in values education!
